Kejardosen : Aplikasi Penjadwalan Bimbingan Tugas Akhir

Project information

  • Use: Html, Css, JavaScript, Bootstrap, jQuery, Laravel, PHP, and others

Final Project Guidance Scheduling App is a handy digital tool designed to make it easy for students and advisors to book and manage their meetings while working on final projects. It streamlines communication and helps both sides manage their time better.
